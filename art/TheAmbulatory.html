The Ambulatory

Alan Perry

The Ambulatory seeks to address how a spiritual understanding of technology may inform the human situation between real and virtual worlds. Along formal lines, the visual information is in constant motion and flux while the text competes for the viewer's attention. This reflects the competition for attention that saturates the experience of the virtual. As an immersive video installation, special care has been taken to ensure that the piece is experienced as a window into a virtual world filled with mystical iconography and historical references.
